Our Story

Thrift, but make it boutique.

Thrif Me Up started as an Instagram page in Pakistan, sharing hand-picked secondhand finds with people who wanted style without the price tag — or the waste. We're now building that same care into a full online store.

What We Believe

Sustainable style, done right

Less Waste

Every piece we sell is one already made — no new resources spent, no landfill added.

Hand-Checked Quality

We inspect fabric, stitching, and wear on every single item before it's listed.

Honest Prices

Boutique curation shouldn't mean boutique prices. We keep things fair, always.

Made for Pakistan

Built by and for the local community, with nationwide delivery for Rs. 250 flat.
